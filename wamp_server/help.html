Help Page

Thanks for using Revolution D! This page is to help you navigate our various systems, providing information and instructions on how to use each part.

1. How to use the device

To begin tracking your blood glucose, you will need our device as well as our app on your mobile phone.
Begin by wearing the device on either hand, connecting the Galvanic Skin Response and Heart Rate sensors to your fingers.

Afterwards, follow the instructions on the app to pair the device with the phone.

Once that is done, the device is ready to start tracking! Just get moving and once you start sweating you'll receive your blood glucose levels through the app.

2. How to see your data

To view a summary your blood glucose data, you can use either this website or your mobile application.

During your exercise your phone will already be giving you information as you exercise.

3. What do I do if I feel unwell during my exercise?

If during your exercise you feel unwell or experience any discomfort, immedietaly seek medical attention. Our device is not a subsitute for trained medical professionals.
